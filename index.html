Idaho

Idaho, a northwestern state in the U.S., is renowned for its rugged landscapes, outdoor activities like skiing and hiking, and its capital, Boise, which features a growing tech sector and cultural amenities. Known as the "Gem State" for its natural resources, particularly potatoes, Idaho offers a mix of natural beauty and urban vibrancy.
